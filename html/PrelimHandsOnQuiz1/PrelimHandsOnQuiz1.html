Crossroad Sangandaan

Quezon City

Crossroad Park Tandang Sora is a bustling shopping mall located at 610 Tandang Sora Avenue, Sangandaan, Novaliches, Quezon City, Philippines. This 13,000-square-meter development offers a variety of retail stores, dining options, and \ essential services, catering to the diverse needs of the local community.

Crossroad Tandang Sora was established in 2015 as a community hub designed to bring convenience and accessibility to the residents of Sangandaan, Quezon City. The shopping complex was developed and is currently owned by the Fortless Land, a local real estate company focused on commercial and transport-oriented spaces.
